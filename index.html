Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a staple in west African cuisine.it's made from rice, tomatoes, onions, peppers and other seasonings.

The dish is cooked in one pot. it's simple and easy to make at home and the end result is absolutely delicious.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 45mins
  • Cook Time: 1hr
  • Servings: 6-8people

Difficulty : if not steamed at medium heat, the lower portion of the rice in the pot becomes soft, while the upper portion will be hard.

Ingredients

    For the Tomatoes Base(Pepper Mix)

  • 4 large red tomatoes
  • 3 red bell pepper(tatashe)
  • 2 scotch bonnet pepper(ata rodo)
  • 1 large onion(for blending)
  • 1 small onion(for frying)

    • For Rice

  • 4 cups(Rice of Choice)
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 bay leaves and 2-3 cups of chicken stock
  • Thyme,curry powder,seasoning of choice
  • 4 sachets of tomatoes paste
  • Butter(optional) , salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash everything listed for the pepper mix and blend smoothly
  2. Parboil or wash the rice, then set aside
  3. Fry the base
  4. Steam and finish
  5. Serve

Chef Tips

  1. Use low heat to achieve party style smokey flavours
  2. Use foil to trap heat and give well steamed rice
  3. Fry your pepper very well.This is where the flavour lives
  4. Stock is better than plain water for richness
